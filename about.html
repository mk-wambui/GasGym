ABOUT GasGYM

OUR story

GasGym was founded in 2019 in the heart of Nairobi by fitness enthusiast Wambui. What started as a small training space with 10 members has grown into one of Nairobi's most vibrant fitness communities with over 500 active members.

Our mission is simple,make world-class fitness accessible to every Nairobian regardless of their fitness level or background. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned athlete, GasGym is your home.
